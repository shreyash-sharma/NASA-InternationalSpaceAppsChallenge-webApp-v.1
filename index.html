Welcome to the Largest Global Hackathon on Earth!

NASA International SpaceApps Challenge Limerick 2021 is brought to you by the Department of Computer Science and Information Systems, University of Limerick. This local event (Limerick) is a 48 Hour International hackathon that will take place virtually from Oct 1st to Oct 3rd, 2021

Who Can Participate?

We warmly welcome all creators, innovators, designers, thinkers, and doers to be a part of this incredible odyssey. You don’t have to be exceptionally great at math or coding!

Over the last few years of Space Apps, Limerick has seen participation from Space Enthusiasts, Programmers, Designers, Architects, Problem Solvers, Developers, Coders, Scientists, Storytellers, Technologists, and more.

If you're passionate about making a difference for the betterment of this planet, we urge you to share your knowledge, skills, and experience with participating communities.

Space Apps ChampionsWhat Will You Win!

Space Apps 2021 winners will be featured on the Space Apps website and social media.

They will be invited to present their projects to NASA personnel (and space agency partners, if applicable).

Our Limerick Champions will receive local event prizes (stay tuned for more on this!).

If travel is deemed safe, the winners will also be invited to visit a NASA site with the Space Apps Global Organizing (GO) Team to view a spacecraft launch. Each member of each winning team and one guest will be invited and will be responsible for covering their own costs for their personal travel, meals and incidental expenses, and accommodation expenses. Winners typically conduct fundraising activities to cover the costs of their trips.

Event Itinerary


1st October 2021
Day 1
6 PM
Pre-hackathon brief. Some important rules, links, and resources of the competition will be discussed.
6:30 PM
NASA SpaceApps 2021 would be explained and problem statements, NASA Open Data and the judging criteria would be introduced.
7 PM
First, it is explained, how to give a pitch, then each participant is welcome to pitch their idea to the audience.
8 PM
Ice-Breaking Sessions using virtual and timed breakout rooms.
9 PM
Hacking begins. Participants are expected to start brainstorming with their teams to hack the chosen problem
11 PM
Take a break; have dinner, fuel up.
2nd October 2021
Day 2
10 AM
Mentors from diverse backgrounds will be accessible to participants through an appointment system and breakout room visitations.
11 AM
Keep hacking
11 PM
Mid Night Munchies: drop down to UL courtyard for some coffee, sandwiches, and sleep-deprived, socially distanced networking.
3rd October 2021
Day 3
6 AM
The final Team List will be prepared for scheduling the presentations.
9 AM
Finalise presentations. This is when you submit a PDF which will be reviewed by the judges while the presentation is going on.
10 AM
Time will be given to help the hackers with their mock presentation.
2 PM
Time for the participants to show off their hard work done during the Space Apps Challenge!
5 PM
Judges Deliberation and Feedback Session. Participants will be reminded to focus on giving kind, specific, and helpful feedback
6 PM
Closing Ceremony and Results. The most awaited moment! The winners are announced, along with the next step.
